Welcome to Malta

A gem in the Mediterranean

Boasting a rich cultural heritage, spectacular archeaological sites, incredible beaches and divine food, the archipelago of Malta has much to offer. Situated between the island of Sicily and the North African coast, Malta's strategic location has played a vital role in shaping its history, as centuries of foreign rule by the Phoenicians, Romans, Arabs, Normans, Knights of St John, French and British determined its fate. And thus, this medley of cultural influences left their mark on Malta's architecture, culture and culinary traditions.
